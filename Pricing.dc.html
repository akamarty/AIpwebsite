FAQ

Questions, answered

Where does the option-code data come from?

Every explanation is generated against factory build-sheet data and cross-checked option by option — not a generic acronym list. Coverage spans every 911, 718, and classic chassis code we track.

Why not just read the factory build sheet myself?

You can — if you already know what X51 or PTS means. sophiapilot reads the codes and explains, in plain English, what each one actually did to the car and why it matters to a buyer or collector, in seconds instead of a forum search.

Is the free plan actually useful?

Yes — unlimited VIN decodes, every factory option explained, the full spec sheet, and a personal Garage to save cars, free forever. Pro and Collector unlock Collector Score, Market Value, and Auction History.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, in one click from your account. You keep access through the end of your billing period.

What if my VIN's model or chassis isn't supported yet?

Collector members can request any VIN and we add full coverage, options included, within 7 days.
